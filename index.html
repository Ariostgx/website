Shuhan Tan

I am currently a senior undergraduate student in School of Data and Computer Science, Sun Yat-Sen University. I am currently working with Prof. Bolei Zhou as a remote research assistant.

I was a research intern at Uber ATG for 11 months, advised by Prof. Raquel Urtasun and Prof. Shenlong Wang. Previously, I was very fortunate to have worked with Prof. Kate Saenko and Dr. Xingchao Peng at Boston University and Prof. Wei-Shi Zheng at iSEE, Sun Yat-sen University.

My research interests lie on Machine Learning, Computer Vision and Robotics Learning. I am interested in tackling problems with real-world variance with adaptive vision learning. Currently, my focus is on learning transferable and generalizable models with the help of simulation and transfer learning.
